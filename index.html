Our Donate Plan

We are focusing on the basic needs to be fulfilled for each and every person, by providing food, cloths, shelter for living, education for children and first aid treatment for all.

Our mission is to provide mobility equipment to those with physical needs and enable them to lead a more active and independent life as well as distribute winter blankets to the poorest of communities and also empower young women with sewing machines to help them become economically independent.

Sylhet Aid is a programme of 13 Rivers Trust and last Ramadan with your generous donations our team were able to deliver enough essential food items to feed 1,200 families! This year we aim to distribute 2,000 food packs to some of the poorest families in Sylhet.

Some of our brothers and sisters are rapidly disappearing due to return migration and natural causes. Sylhet Aid is continuing the good work of our elders by providing support to the most needy. Our aim is to deliver aid to the poorest communities in the greater Sylhet region.
